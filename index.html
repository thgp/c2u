About Us

It's all about the food


Catered To You, located in Oakland, CA opened its doors on May 8th, 2008, owned and operated by Teena Johnson.

Ms. Johnson is passionate about bringing phenomenal cuisine to her customers and giving back to the community when possible.

She has been cooking for over 40 years, of which 22 years professionally.


Catered To You opened with the desire to bring good food and value to the Oakland community and has been doing so for over 5 years.

Having survived the economic downfall of late 2008 and beyond, Catered To You has become household name in the downtown Oakland area.

Catered To You specializes in american and cajun cuisine, providing a daily menu from its Telegraph Ave store front, but also caters all types of events including formal wedding receptions to a casual bar-b-que, or perhaps an intimate dinner for two.

Drop by and check us out, we would love to share our love for food with you.
